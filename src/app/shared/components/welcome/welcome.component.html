Objective
The ALIENNOR platform is developed in the card of the ALIENNOR project. The overall objective of this project is to develop a method to support companies in their innovation process, with the aim of bringing products or services with strong environmental and societal ambitions to the market. The ALIENNOR method is used to guide the company through tools adapted to the entire upstream innovation process, up to the exhaustive characterization of a mature eco-innovative concept. The overall objective of this project is to develop a method to support companies in their innovation process, with the aim of bringing products or services with strong environmental and societal ambitions to the market. The ALIENNOR method is used to guide the company through tools adapted to the entire upstream innovation process, up to the exhaustive characterization of a mature eco-innovative concept. Thus, the method proposed in this research program is systematically applicable, and should lead to mature eco-innovations, acting on three levels: mature, acting on three levels:
  • The idea generation phase, with a toolbox of stimulation mechanisms to help the actors to consider the possible futures.
  • The evaluation of eco-innovations, in particular by helping to choose the most promising concepts according to several relevant indicators in this context (technical performance, sustainability of the associated business model, environmental relevance over a long period of time, solidity of the network of stakeholders ).
  • The coordination of the idea generation and concept evaluation phases: this involves formalizing the eco-innovation process and illustrating it through a directory of eco-innovation cases.
5 Workpackages
  • WP01: analysis of contradictions on the basis of eco-innovation cases.
  • WP02: development of a set of mechanisms for stimulation of eco-ideation (MSE) exhaustive and easily operated by companies.
  • WP03: formalization of a grid of choice of the MSE usable by case.
  • WP04: development and evaluation of ideas
  • WP05: development of the ALIENNOR platform
